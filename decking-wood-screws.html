Decking Wood Screws

Decking wood screws are specially designed for outdoor applications like decking, fencing and wooden flooring systems. These screws offer excellent weather resistance, strong grip and long-lasting durability, even in harsh environmental conditions.

Klimb Fasteners decking screws ensure secure fixing, reduced loosening and superior performance in outdoor wood structures.

WKFC + WKFS

WKFC ensures a neat finish.

WKFS provides strong structural grip.

WKFC screws are ideal for clean finishing in visible decking areas, while WKFS screws are designed for strong structural applications requiring high holding strength.

WKPC + WKLC

WKPC is for panel fixing.

WKLC supports light cladding applications.

WKPC screws are used for firm fixing in decking panels, while WKLC screws are suitable for light wooden cladding and general outdoor use.
